Hi, I’m Bisceglia.

All Things Bee began from a simple idea: words have the power to stay with people.

I wanted to create greeting cards that felt intentional — cards that encourage, celebrate, comfort, and remind people they are seen.

Every design is created with warmth, elegance, and meaningful connection at the center. My hope is that each card feels personal enough to keep long after the moment has passed.
